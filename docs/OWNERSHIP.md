# Ownership Boundaries

## Human project owner
- Final product priorities and acceptance.
- Merge and deployment authorization.
- Production access approval.
- Vehicle installation and road-test approval.
- Acceptance of physical and safety evidence.

## Chief engineer agent
- Requirements, decomposition, architecture, dependencies, and risk framing.
- May propose decisions; may not silently make product or safety decisions.

## Specialist agents
- Implement bounded issue scope.
- Provide validation evidence and draft pull requests.
- Stop at unresolved cross-domain decisions.

## Independent reviewer
- Reviews scope, checks, risk, evidence, and rollback.
- Does not rely solely on the implementing agent's summary.
